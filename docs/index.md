## Welcome to DevNotes

This is your personal, all-in-one knowledge base. Everything is built to be:

1. **Categorized** — Languages and Courses as top-level sections.
2. **Step by step** — each topic breaks down into clear steps.
3. **Editable** — use the [Live Editor](/editor) to draft and save notes instantly.
4. **Portable** — export to `.md` and commit to your repo.

> Get started: pick a language from the sidebar, or open the editor to write your own.
