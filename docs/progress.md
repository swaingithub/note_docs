## How progress works

1. Open a **course index** → the "Course Checklist" tracks every lesson.
2. Open a **lesson** → its own "Checklist" tracks specific tasks.
3. Everything is saved automatically in your browser. Click **Reset** to clear.

> Tip: Use the [Live Editor](/editor) alongside lessons to draft notes, then export them into a page.
